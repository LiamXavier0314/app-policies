Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Manna ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our offline Bible widget application.

Manna is designed with privacy as a core principle. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Manna is a completely offline application that:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not access your contacts, location, or other device data

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, share, or process any personal data.

4. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Bible content downloaded from BibleGateway (CUV) and GitHub Bible Database
  • Your theme preferences
  • Your font style selections
  • Your custom background images

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

5. Image Sharing Feature

Manna allows you to create and share Bible verse images. When you use the share function:

  • Images are generated locally on your device
  • Images are not uploaded to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Sharing is handled entirely by iOS's native sharing system
  • You control where and how images are shared

6. In-App Purchases

Manna offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium features including:

  • Additional themes
  • Custom font styles
  • Custom background images

All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles your payment data.

7. Third-Party Services

Manna does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Bible content is sourced from:

  • BibleGateway - Chinese Union Version (CUV)
  • GitHub - Bible Database (scrollmapper/bible_databases)

This content is downloaded once and stored locally. No ongoing connection to these services is maintained during normal app usage.

8. Children's Privacy

Manna is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your iOS updated
  • Using device encryption (enabled by default on iOS)

10. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfers.

11. Your Rights

Under various privacy laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and others), you have certain rights regarding your personal data. However, since we do not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply to our app.

You have complete control over all app data stored on your device. You can:

  • Delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Reset app settings within the app
  • Manage your purchase history through your Apple ID

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

13. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) - China
  • Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) - Brazil
  • Other applicable privacy laws in regions where the app is distributed
