Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Manna ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our offline Bible widget application.

Manna is designed with privacy as a core principle. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Manna is primarily an offline application. However, we use a limited third-party service (RevenueCat) solely for validating in-app purchases.

2.1 Information Collected by Us

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage or behavior tracking data
  • Contacts, location, or other device data

2.2 Information Collected by RevenueCat (Purchase Validation Only)

When you make an in-app purchase, RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous device identifier (not linked to your identity)
  • Purchase receipt data from Apple (to verify your purchase)
  • App version and platform information (iOS version)

Important: This data is used ONLY to validate your purchase and ensure you have access to premium features. RevenueCat does NOT process payments—all payments are handled by Apple. RevenueCat does NOT have access to your payment information or personal data.

2.3 Specific Data We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent, here is a detailed list of information we do NOT collect:

  • IP address or precise time zone
  • Device location (general or precise)
  • Crash logs or diagnostic information
  • Performance data (app launch time, hang rate, energy usage)
  • User behavior or usage patterns
  • Attribution data from advertising sources
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, share, or process any personal data.

4. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Bible content downloaded from BibleGateway (CUV) and GitHub Bible Database
  • Your theme preferences
  • Your font style selections
  • Your custom background images

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

5. Image Sharing Feature

Manna allows you to create and share Bible verse images. When you use the share function:

  • Images are generated locally on your device
  • Images are not uploaded to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Sharing is handled entirely by iOS's native sharing system
  • You control where and how images are shared

6. In-App Purchases

Manna offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium features including:

  • Additional themes
  • Custom font styles
  • Custom background images

6.1 Payment Processing

All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles your payment data.

6.2 Purchase Management Service (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage and validate your purchase status. RevenueCat does NOT process payments—all payments are handled by Apple. RevenueCat only helps us verify your purchase and ensure you have access to premium features.

RevenueCat may collect the following information:

  • Anonymous device identifier (not linked to your personal identity)
  • Purchase receipt data from Apple (to verify your purchase)
  • App version and platform information

Important: RevenueCat does NOT have access to:

  • Your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc. - handled exclusively by Apple)
  • Your Bible reading data or any content you enter in the app
  • Your personal information or identity

RevenueCat uses this data solely to validate your purchase status and ensure you have access to premium features. For more information about how RevenueCat handles data, please visit their privacy policy at: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

7. Tracking Technologies

We do not use any tracking technologies.

Specifically, Manna does NOT use:

  • Advertising Identifiers: We do not access or use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any other device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Cookies: We do not send or store any cookies on your device
  • Analytics Tools: We do not use any analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services)
  • Tracking Pixels: We do not use any tracking pixels or beacons
  • Fingerprinting: We do not collect device fingerprints or use any fingerprinting techniques

Since we do not track you in any way, there is no need to opt out of tracking. The app does not respond to Do-Not-Track (DNT) signals because it does not track you in the first place.

8. Service Providers

We use a limited number of third-party service providers to help us operate the app:

8.1 RevenueCat (Purchase Validation)

We use RevenueCat to validate purchase receipts and manage your access to premium features. RevenueCat does NOT process payments—all payments are handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store.

RevenueCat has access to:

  • Anonymous device identifiers
  • Purchase receipt data from Apple
  • App version and platform data

RevenueCat does NOT have access to your Bible reading data, personal information, or payment details. For more information, see their privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

8.2 Apple Inc. (Payment Processing)

Apple processes all payment transactions through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

8.3 What We Do NOT Use

Manna does NOT use third-party services for:

  • Analytics or user behavior tracking
  • Advertising or marketing
  • Cloud storage or data hosting
  • Crash reporting or diagnostics
  • Social media integration

9. Third-Party Services and SDKs

Manna integrates with the following third-party services:

9.1 RevenueCat SDK

We use the RevenueCat SDK to validate in-app purchases. This SDK collects minimal data as described in Section 8.1 above. RevenueCat does NOT process payments—it only validates purchase receipts from Apple.

9.2 Bible Content Sources

The Bible content in Manna is sourced from the following open-source resources:

  • BibleGateway - Chinese Union Version Simplified (CUVS)
    Source: https://www.biblegateway.com/versions/Chinese-Union-Version-Simplified-CUVS/
    The Chinese Union Version is in the public domain and freely available for use.
  • GitHub - Bible Database (scrollmapper/bible_databases)
    Source: https://github.com/scrollmapper/bible_databases
    This open-source database provides structured Bible text data available for personal and commercial use.

This content is downloaded once during app installation and stored locally on your device. No ongoing connection to these services is maintained during normal app usage. We do not track or monitor your Bible reading activity.

9.3 What We Do NOT Use

Manna does NOT integrate with:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
  • Cloud storage services (iCloud sync is optional and controlled by iOS)
  • Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Any other data collection or tracking services

10. Children's Privacy

Manna is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take all reasonable steps to delete it as soon as possible. However, given our offline-first design, this scenario is highly unlikely.

11. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your iOS updated
  • Using device encryption (enabled by default on iOS)

12. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfers.

13. Your Rights Under Privacy Laws

13.1 Privacy Rights of Users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information (all data is already accessible within the app)
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections of inaccurate personal information (you can edit data within the app)
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information (you can delete the app)
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of personal information processing (not applicable as we don't process data)
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a portable copy of your personal information (you can share images)
  • Right to Object: Object to personal information processing (not applicable as we don't process data)
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Since we do not collect or process any personal data, you can exercise these rights directly on your device without contacting us. However, if you have questions, you can contact us at the email provided below.

13.2 Privacy Rights of California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, you have the following privacy rights:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of how we collect, use, and share your personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out from the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Limit Use: Limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

Important Disclosures for California Residents:

  • In the preceding 12 months, we have NOT collected any personal information
  • In the preceding 12 months, we have NOT sold any personal information
  • In the preceding 12 months, we have NOT shared personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • We do NOT sell or share the personal information of minors under 16 years of age
  • In the preceding 12 months, we have NOT disclosed any personal information for business purposes

Since we do not collect any personal information, these rights are automatically protected. All your data remains on your device under your complete control.

13.3 Other Privacy Rights

Under various other privacy laws (PIPL in China, LGPD in Brazil, and others), you have similar rights. Since we do not collect any personal information, you have complete control over all data stored on your device:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Right to Rectification: You can reset app settings within the app
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can share Bible verse images
  • Right to Object: Not applicable as we don't process your data
