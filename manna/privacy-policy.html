Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Manna is designed to be local-first: verse content, favorites, widget settings, reading progress, and reminder preferences are primarily stored on your device. We do not require an account to use the app.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Manna ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information when you use the app and its widget features.

Manna is an iOS-first daily verse and widget app with offline content, local personalization, optional notifications, optional custom backgrounds, and premium theme purchases.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Manna stores app data locally on your device, which may include:

  • Theme, font, color, and layout preferences
  • Favorite verses and pinned verses
  • Onboarding status, language preferences, and bilingual mode settings
  • Reading plan progress, devotional completion state, and revisit flow state
  • Reminder settings and the time slot you selected
  • Premium entitlement status needed for app behavior on your device
  • Paths to custom background images you choose to use

3. Widget Data and Shared App Group Storage

To power the iOS home screen widget, Manna stores selected widget data in the iOS App Group container used by the main app and widget extension. This may include:

  • Verse text, reference, language, and styling choices
  • Widget theme, font, and color configuration
  • Locally copied verse data used by the widget
  • Background image files copied for widget rendering

This storage is intended to remain on your device and is used so the widget can render content independently from the main app.

4. Data We Do Not Collect for Our Own Servers

We do not require account registration. We do not run our own analytics, advertising, or social-tracking systems for your reading activity, favorites, reminders, or widget usage.

We do not intentionally collect your contacts, precise location, microphone input, or camera recordings for Manna's core experience.

5. Photo Library Access

Manna may request photo library access so you can select a custom background image or save/share verse images. If you deny this permission, those related features may not work.

Selected background images are stored locally and may also be copied into the app group container so the widget can display them.

6. Notifications and Reminders

If you enable reminders, Manna requests local notification permission from iOS and schedules reminders on your device. Reminder settings and related state are stored locally.

We do not need an account to send these reminders because they are local device notifications, not a server-driven push system operated by us.

7. Sharing and Generated Images

Manna lets you generate and share verse images. These images are rendered locally on your device and then passed to Apple's native share mechanisms. Once you share content to another app or service, that destination's privacy practices apply.

8. In-App Purchases and Service Providers

Manna uses RevenueCat together with Apple's in-app purchase infrastructure to validate premium access, fetch offerings, complete purchases, and restore entitlements.

Apple and RevenueCat may process limited commerce-related information such as anonymous device identifiers, purchase receipts, app version, and entitlement status. We do not send your favorite verses, devotional progress, or personal widget content to our own servers as part of this process.

You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

9. Content Delivery

Manna's verse, devotional, and reading-plan experience is primarily backed by bundled or locally stored content rather than a general online content account system. Your reading activity is intended to remain on-device.

10. Siri and System Suggestions

Manna may donate limited app activity to iOS system features such as search or suggestions so the app can integrate better with platform experiences. This behavior is controlled by Apple system frameworks.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

Your local Manna data remains on your device until you remove it, reset it, or uninstall the app. If you save or share generated verse images, those copies may remain wherever you stored or shared them.

12. Children's Privacy

Manna is not built around user accounts or direct submission of personal information to us. If you believe a child submitted personal information to us directly, contact us so we can review the situation.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date and publish the new version here.
