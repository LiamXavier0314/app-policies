Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the Manna widget to my home screen?

On iPhone: Long press on your home screen ‚Üí tap the "+" button in the top-left corner ‚Üí search for "Manna" ‚Üí choose your preferred widget size (small, medium, or large) ‚Üí tap "Add Widget" ‚Üí position it where you like.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the Manna app ‚Üí go to Settings ‚Üí tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your premium features will be restored automatically.

Does Manna require an internet connection?

No. Manna is designed as an offline-first app. All Bible content is stored locally on your device. You can use all features ‚Äî widgets, themes, and image sharing ‚Äî without any internet connection.

Does Manna collect my personal data?

No. Manna does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking. All your data (preferences, themes, etc.) stays on your device. The only third-party service used is RevenueCat, solely for validating in-app purchases.

How do I change the Bible verse displayed on the widget?

The widget automatically updates with a new verse daily. You can also open the Manna app to manually refresh or select a specific verse.

How do I change the widget theme?

Open the Manna app ‚Üí browse the theme gallery ‚Üí tap on a theme to preview it ‚Üí apply it to your widget. Free users have access to 6 themes; premium users can use all 30+ themes.

How do I share a Bible verse as an image?

Open the Manna app ‚Üí select a verse ‚Üí tap the share button ‚Üí the verse image is generated locally on your device ‚Üí choose where to share it via the iOS share sheet. No data is uploaded to any server.

What Bible versions are included?

Manna includes curated verses from the KJV (King James Version, public domain) and the Chinese Union Version (CUV, public domain). All content is pre-loaded and available offline.

The widget is not updating. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Remove the widget and re-add it to your home screen
  • Make sure Manna is not restricted in Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Background App Refresh
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you are running the latest version of Manna from the App Store

I purchased premium but my themes are not unlocked.

Please try the following:

  • Go to Settings in the Manna app and tap "Restore Purchases"
  • Make sure you are signed in with the correct Apple ID
  • Check your internet connection (needed only for purchase validation)
  • If the issue persists, contact us at onebestai@gmail.com with your Apple ID and purchase receipt

Is Manna safe for children?

Yes. Manna is rated 4+ on the App Store. It contains no ads, no social features, no user-generated content, and no external links within the app. It is safe for users of all ages.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, simply deleting the Manna app will remove all associated data. There is no cloud data to worry about.
