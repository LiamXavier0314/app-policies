Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Your Privacy is Our Priority: CycleSafe is designed to be completely private and secure. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or health data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CycleSafe ("we," "our," or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our offline period tracking application.

CycleSafe is built with privacy and security as fundamental principles. We understand that menstrual health data is highly sensitive and personal, which is why we've designed the app to keep all your information completely private and under your control.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health information.

CycleSafe is a completely offline application that:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not collect any health data
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not access your contacts, location, photos, or other device data
  • Does not connect to the internet

3. Health Data Storage

All health and personal data is stored exclusively on your device, including:

  • Menstrual cycle dates and duration
  • Symptoms and notes
  • Mood tracking
  • Flow intensity
  • Any other information you choose to record

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us, third parties, or anyone else.

4. Data Security Features

CycleSafe includes premium security features to protect your privacy:

4.1 Biometric Lock (Premium Feature)

  • Face ID or Touch ID protection
  • Prevents unauthorized access to your data
  • Biometric data is processed by iOS and never accessed by our app

4.2 Passcode Lock (Premium Feature)

  • Custom passcode protection
  • Passcode is stored locally on your device
  • We cannot recover your passcode if you forget it

4.3 Disguise Mode (Premium Feature)

  • Calculator disguise to hide the app's true purpose
  • Protects your privacy from casual observers
  • No data is transmitted when using disguise mode

4.4 Quick Lock (Premium Feature)

  • Flip your phone to instantly lock the app
  • Uses device motion sensors (processed locally)
  • Motion data is not stored or transmitted

5. Data Export

CycleSafe allows you to export your data as a PDF file. When you use the export function:

  • PDF files are generated locally on your device
  • Files are not uploaded to our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Sharing is handled entirely by iOS's native sharing system
  • You control where and how files are shared
  • We recommend encrypting or password-protecting exported files if sharing

6. Apple Health Integration

CycleSafe does not integrate with Apple Health or HealthKit. All data remains exclusively within the app.

7. In-App Purchases

CycleSafe offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium security features:

  • Biometric lock (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • Passcode protection
  • Disguise mode (calculator)
  • Quick lock (flip to lock)

All purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles your payment data.

8. Third-Party Services

CycleSafe does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud storage services.

The app does not use any third-party SDKs or libraries that collect data.

9. Children's Privacy

CycleSafe is rated 12+ and is intended for users who have reached puberty. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for minors' data. However, we recommend that parents or guardians discuss the use of period tracking apps with younger users.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete individual entries within the app
  • You can reset all data within the app settings
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all data from your device
  • We cannot recover deleted data as we never had access to it

11. Data Backup

If you use iCloud Backup or iTunes Backup:

  • Your CycleSafe data may be included in device backups
  • This is controlled by iOS, not by our app
  • Backup data is encrypted by Apple
  • We do not have access to your backups
  • You can exclude CycleSafe from backups in iOS settings if desired

12. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfers. All data remains on your device regardless of your location.

13. Your Rights Under Privacy Laws

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, PIPL, LGPD, and others), you have certain rights regarding your personal data. However, since we do not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply in the traditional sense.

You have complete control over all data stored on your device:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit any data within the app
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete data or uninstall the app
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data as PDF
  • Right to Object: Not applicable as we don't process your data

14. Security Recommendations

To maximize your privacy and security, we recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID on your device
  • Keeping your iOS updated to the latest version
  • Using the premium lock features within CycleSafe
  • Being cautious when exporting and sharing data
  • Not sharing your device with others

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

16. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) - China
  • Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) - Brazil
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) principles
  • Other applicable privacy and health data laws worldwide

17. Transparency Commitment

We are committed to complete transparency about our privacy practices. If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data (or rather, how we don't handle it), please don't hesitate to contact us.
