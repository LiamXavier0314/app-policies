Getting Started

First Time Setup

  1. Download and install PetalCare from the App Store
  2. Open the app and grant necessary permissions (notifications, if desired)
  3. Start tracking by adding your first period date
  4. Explore premium security features (optional)

System Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • Compatible with iPhone and iPad
  • Approximately 50MB of storage space
  • No internet connection required (fully offline)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I track my period?

Tap the calendar icon and select the start date of your period. Mark each day of your cycle, and the app will automatically calculate your cycle length and predict future periods.

Q: How accurate are the predictions?

Predictions are based on your historical data and become more accurate over time as you track more cycles. However, many factors can affect your cycle, so predictions should be used as estimates only.

Q: Is my data safe and private?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or health information. For additional security, consider using our premium lock features.

Q: How do I enable Face ID/Touch ID lock?

This is a premium feature. After purchasing, go to Settings → Security → Enable Biometric Lock. You'll need to have Face ID or Touch ID enabled on your device.

Q: I forgot my passcode. How can I recover it?

For security reasons, we cannot recover your passcode. If you forget it, you'll need to reset the app, which will delete all local data. We recommend using Face ID/Touch ID as a backup authentication method.

Q: How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data → Generate PDF. You can then share or save the PDF file using iOS's native sharing options.

Q: Can I sync my data across multiple devices?

Currently, PetalCare does not support cloud sync to maintain maximum privacy. Your data is stored locally on each device. You can use the export feature to manually transfer data between devices.

Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Install PetalCare on your new device, go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're using the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.

Q: What is Disguise Mode?

Disguise Mode (premium feature) transforms the app interface into a calculator, protecting your privacy from casual observers. Enable it in Settings → Security → Disguise Mode.

Q: How does Quick Lock work?

Quick Lock (premium feature) allows you to instantly lock the app by flipping your phone face-down. Enable it in Settings → Security → Quick Lock.

Troubleshooting

App Won't Open or Crashes

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for iOS updates in Settings → General → Software Update
  4. Reinstall the app (note: this will delete local data unless backed up via iOS backup)

Face ID/Touch ID Not Working

  1. Ensure Face ID/Touch ID is enabled in iOS Settings → Face ID & Passcode
  2. Check that PetalCare has permission to use biometric authentication
  3. Try disabling and re-enabling the feature in app settings
  4. Restart your device

Notifications Not Appearing

  1. Go to iOS Settings → Notifications → PetalCare
  2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  3. Check notification settings within the app
  4. Restart your device

Data Not Saving

  1. Check available storage space on your device
  2. Ensure the app has necessary permissions
  3. Try force closing and reopening the app
  4. If the issue persists, contact support

Purchase Issues

  1. Verify your payment method in App Store settings
  2. Try restoring your purchase: Settings → Restore Purchase
  3. Sign out and back into your Apple ID
  4. Contact Apple Support for payment-related issues

Data Backup & Recovery

Backing Up Your Data

PetalCare data is automatically included in your iOS device backup (iCloud or iTunes). To ensure your data is backed up:

  1. For iCloud: Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → Enable
  2. For iTunes: Connect to computer and create a backup via iTunes/Finder

Restoring Your Data

When you restore your device from an iCloud or iTunes backup, your PetalCare data will be restored automatically.
